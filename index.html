The Space Learning System is an application for the augmented reality headset, Meta.
This allows its users to navigate through the solar system by reaching out and grabbing the artificially augmented planets, while the interface displays facts about the selected planets. It is based on the concept of using new cutting-edge technology to explore the beauty of outer space, and is beneficial to anyone interested in the wonders of the space around us.
Our team consists of students solely from Oakland University, all members of LECS.
The undergraduate students were successfully able to develop an interactive space exploring learning tool by manipulating reality in front of the user's eyes.
